01

Overview

Simple Spirit Level is a precision measurement tool. It uses your phone's accelerometer to display tilt angles. That is the entire scope of what this app does.

This policy describes what data the app accesses, how it is used, and confirms that no information is collected, transmitted or shared with any third party.

02

Data we collect

None. Simple Spirit Level does not collect any personal data, usage data, diagnostic data, or any other information about you or your device.

03

Permissions used

The app requests the following Android permissions:

Permission Why it's needed
ACCELEROMETER Reads tilt angles from the device sensor to display bubble level readings. No data leaves the app.
VIBRATE Powers the optional haptic feedback when you approach level. Only triggers if you enable it in settings.

The app does not request internet, location, camera, microphone, contacts, storage, or any other permission.

04

Local storage

Your saved angles and settings (dark mode, colour mode, skin, calibration offsets) are stored locally on your device using Android SharedPreferences. This data never leaves your device and is deleted if you uninstall the app.

05

Third-party services

Simple Spirit Level contains no third-party SDKs, analytics libraries, advertising networks, or crash reporting services. There are no external network requests of any kind.

06

Children's privacy

This app does not collect data from anyone, including children under 13. It is rated Everyone on Google Play.

07

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a new effective date. Given the nature of the app, significant changes are unlikely.
